 How our products are manufactured 


Srilankan Traditional Clay Pots






Clay pots provide Phosphorus, Iron, Magnesium, and a variety of other minerals. Clay's alkaline nature aids in the neutralization of the food's pH balance by interacting with the acid present in the food. Because clay is porous, it allows heat and moisture to circulate evenly, making food juicer and cooking more evenly. We guarantee that no chemicals were used in the production of this product. It is made from clay that has been excavated naturally. We season the pot with edible corn starch. Due to temperature changes, red pots may turn black and black pots may turn red from the bottom while cooing. It is a centuries-old pottery technique. A red clay vessel is kilned only once. Deep Burned Pots are kilned two times.

Srilankan Traditional Wooden Mask






These masks are suitable for usage both indoors and outdoors. Perfect for any event, party, or business setting. Since its inception, this workmanship has been passed down through the generations within the same families. The Raksha masks are constructed of kadura, a light, soft wood that is smoke-dried before carving, and the wood is light and sturdy. All of the masks on display and for sale have been painstakingly hand carved with intricate designs and brilliant colors to reflect the traditional heritage.
